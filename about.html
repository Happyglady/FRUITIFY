Our Story

From farm to fork, we're revolutionizing how you enjoy nature's candy

Born From A Simple Idea

In 2020, our founder Sarah was tired of choosing between convenience and quality. Supermarket fruits were bland, farmers markets were inconvenient, and delivery services lacked transparency.

So we built Fruitify: a platform that connects you directly with farms, guarantees peak freshness, and delivers within hours — not days.

Today, we partner with 47 farms across 12 countries, serve 50,000+ customers, and have saved over 2 million pounds of food from waste through our smart ripeness matching.

Our Values
